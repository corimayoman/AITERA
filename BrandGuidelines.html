07
Spacing Rule — Clear Space & Minimum Size
avely.ai
Clear space
Minimum clear space = height of the letter "a" in the wordmark on all four sides. Never crowd the logo.
Minimum size
Digital: 120px wide minimum. Print: 30mm wide minimum. Below these sizes, use icon only.
Icon-to-wordmark gap
Gap between icon and wordmark = 1× the icon height. This ratio must be preserved at all scales.

Brand guidelines — avely.ai
Typography system
Outfit 400 — wordmark, UI, body copy
Cormorant Garamond Italic 500 — .ai suffix only

The contrast between geometric sans and refined italic serif signals: structured intelligence meets human insight.
Icon concept
Hexagon = structure, neural network, precision.
A without crossbar = forward movement, no barriers.
Fuchsia apex dot = AI as the catalyst — the point where everything converges.

The dot echoes the brand accent in the wordmark.
